Murder Mafia

Dive into the world of mystery and deception in this thrilling multiplayer murder mystery game.

About the Game

Murder Mafia is an intense multiplayer murder mystery game where players take on different roles in a thrilling game of deception and strategy. As either a detective or a murderer, you must use your wits and observation skills to either catch the killer or eliminate your targets without being discovered.

With its unique gameplay mechanics, atmospheric environments, and strategic elements, Murder Mafia offers an engaging experience that will keep you on the edge of your seat. The game's social deduction aspects make it perfect for playing with friends or making new ones online.

Game Features

  • Multiple player roles and abilities
  • Dynamic environments and hiding spots
  • Real-time multiplayer gameplay
  • Strategic communication system
  • Progression and customization options
  • Regular updates and new content

How to Play

Basic Controls

  • Move WASD or Arrow keys to move
  • Interact E key to interact with objects
  • Chat T key to open chat

Game Rules

The objective varies based on your role:

  • As a detective, find and eliminate the murderer
  • As a murderer, eliminate targets without being caught
  • Use the environment to your advantage
  • Communicate with other players strategically
  • Complete objectives to win the game
