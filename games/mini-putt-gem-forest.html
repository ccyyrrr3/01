Mini Putt Gem Forest

Sports Golf Minigolf

Tee off in a magical forest filled with gems and challenging courses. Perfect your putting skills in this enchanting minigolf adventure!

Game Description

Mini Putt Gem Forest is a delightful minigolf game set in a mystical forest environment. Navigate through beautifully designed courses featuring glowing gems, magical obstacles, and forest-themed hazards.

Each hole presents a unique challenge with varying terrain, slopes, and obstacles. You'll need to calculate the perfect angle and power for each shot to achieve the lowest score possible. Watch out for water hazards, moving platforms, and other tricky elements that add to the challenge.

With intuitive controls and stunning visuals, Mini Putt Gem Forest offers a relaxing yet engaging experience for players of all ages. Whether you're a minigolf pro or a casual player, you'll find this magical forest course both challenging and entertaining.

How to Play

  • Aim: Click and drag to set direction
  • Power: Hold longer for more power
  • Release: Let go to take your shot
  • Reset: R key to restart the hole

Tips:

  • Pay attention to the terrain slopes
  • Use walls to bank your shots when needed
  • Start with lower power for precision
  • Look for shortcuts on complex holes
