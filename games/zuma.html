Zuma

Experience the addictive marble-shooting puzzle game that has captivated millions of players worldwide.

About the Game

Zuma is a classic marble-shooting puzzle game that combines strategy, timing, and precision. The game features a frog that can shoot colored marbles at a chain of marbles moving along a spiral path. Match three or more marbles of the same color to eliminate them and prevent the chain from reaching the skull at the end.

With its addictive gameplay, beautiful graphics, and increasing difficulty levels, Zuma offers an engaging experience that will keep you entertained for hours. The game's simple yet challenging mechanics make it perfect for players of all skill levels.

Game Features

  • Classic Zuma gameplay mechanics
  • Beautiful marble graphics and animations
  • Multiple difficulty levels
  • Strategic power-ups and special marbles
  • Score tracking system
  • Engaging sound effects

How to Play

Basic Controls

  • Aim Move your mouse to aim the frog
  • Shoot Click to shoot the marble
  • Power-ups Collect special marbles for powerful effects

Game Rules

The objective is to clear all marbles before they reach the skull:

  • Match 3 or more marbles of the same color to eliminate them
  • Chain reactions can clear multiple marbles at once
  • Prevent marbles from reaching the skull at the end
  • Use power-ups strategically to clear difficult sections
  • Score points for each marble eliminated
