Idle Saga

Build your empire in this engaging idle game. Manage resources, upgrade buildings, and watch your kingdom grow even when you're away.

About the Game

Idle Saga is a strategic idle game where you build and expand your empire. The game continues to progress even when you're not playing, allowing you to accumulate resources and grow your kingdom over time.

With a variety of buildings to construct, resources to manage, and upgrades to unlock, Idle Saga offers a satisfying progression system that keeps you engaged for hours. Make strategic decisions to optimize your resource production and expand your territory.

Game Features

  • Idle progression system
  • Multiple resource types to manage
  • Various buildings and upgrades
  • Strategic decision-making
  • Offline progression
  • Engaging gameplay loop

How to Play

Basic Controls

  • Click Click on buildings to collect resources
  • Upgrade Click upgrade buttons to improve buildings
  • Build Construct new buildings to expand your empire
  • Manage Balance resource production and consumption

Game Rules

The objective is to build a thriving empire:

  • Collect resources to build and upgrade
  • Balance different resource types
  • Expand your territory strategically
  • Optimize production chains
  • Progress continues even when offline
