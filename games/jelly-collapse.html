Jelly Collapse

Puzzle Match Three Casual

Match and clear colorful jellies in this addictive puzzle game. Connect groups of the same color, create combos, and solve increasingly challenging levels!

Game Description

Jelly Collapse is a delightful and challenging puzzle game that will test your pattern recognition and strategic thinking. The game features a grid filled with colorful jelly blocks in various shapes and colors. Your objective is to match and connect jellies of the same color to make them collapse and disappear from the board.

As you progress through the levels, you'll encounter new challenges, obstacles, and special jellies with unique properties. Some levels have move limits, while others may introduce locked jellies, bombs, or color-changing power-ups that add depth to the gameplay.

With its bright, colorful visuals, satisfying sound effects, and gradually increasing difficulty, Jelly Collapse offers a perfect balance of accessibility and challenge. Whether you have a few minutes to spare or want to enjoy a longer gaming session, this addictive puzzle game will keep you entertained for hours!

How to Play

  • Connect: Click on groups of the same color jellies
  • Match: Need at least 3 jellies of the same color
  • Clear: Remove all jellies to complete the level
  • Power-ups: Create special jellies by making larger matches

Tips:

  • Focus on making larger groups for more points
  • Look for strategic matches that can create chain reactions
  • Prioritize removing jellies that block other potential matches
  • Save power-ups for difficult situations or to earn high scores
