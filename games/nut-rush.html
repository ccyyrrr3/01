Nut Rush

Arcade Action Platform

Join the adventure of a hungry squirrel as it collects nuts and navigates through various obstacles and enemies in this exciting platform game.

Game Description

Nut Rush is an engaging platform game where you control a squirrel on a mission to collect as many nuts as possible. The game features colorful graphics, smooth controls, and increasingly challenging levels that will test your timing and reflexes.

Along your journey, you'll encounter various obstacles and enemies that stand between you and your precious nuts. Use your agility to jump over barriers, dodge enemies, and collect power-ups that can help you on your adventure.

With multiple levels to explore and secrets to discover, Nut Rush offers hours of entertainment for players of all ages. Do you have what it takes to help our squirrel friend collect all the nuts?

How to Play

  • Movement: Arrow keys or WASD
  • Jump: Space bar or Up arrow
  • Crouch: Down arrow
  • Pause: P key

Tips:

  • Try to collect all nuts in each level
  • Timing is crucial when jumping over enemies
  • Some walls can be climbed - experiment!
  • Health power-ups will restore your energy
