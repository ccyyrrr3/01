Poxel.io

Battle other players in this exciting voxel-based multiplayer game with building and combat elements.

About the Game

Poxel.io is a multiplayer voxel-based game where players compete against each other in a colorful, blocky world. Build structures, collect resources, and battle other players in this fast-paced action game that combines elements of building and combat.

With its unique combination of construction and competitive gameplay, Poxel.io offers an engaging experience that rewards both creativity and combat skills. Adapt your strategy on the fly, build defensive structures, or go on the offensive against other players.

Game Features

  • Real-time multiplayer battles
  • Creative building mechanics
  • Various weapons and tools
  • Colorful voxel-based graphics
  • Different game modes
  • Resource collection and management

How to Play

Basic Controls

  • Move WASD or Arrow keys to move
  • Build Left click to place blocks
  • Attack Right click to attack
  • Jump Space bar to jump

Game Rules

Your objective is to dominate the arena:

  • Collect resources from the environment
  • Build structures for defense or tactical advantage
  • Eliminate other players to gain points
  • Survive as long as possible
  • Adapt your strategy based on the game mode

Why Players Love This Game

Creative Freedom

Quick matches and intense combat keep players engaged and coming back for more.

Multiplayer Competition
