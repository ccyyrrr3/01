About Playground

Playground is a physics-based sports game that offers a variety of mini-games and challenges in a colorful, fun environment. Each mini-game tests different skills like timing, precision, and quick reflexes. From basketball shooting to soccer challenges, there's something for everyone!

The game features realistic physics that make each challenge feel authentic, while maintaining a casual and accessible gameplay style that anyone can enjoy.

How to Play

  • Controls: Mouse-based - click and drag to aim and release to perform actions
  • Power: Hold longer for more power in your shots and throws
  • Angle: Adjust your aim by dragging in different directions
  • Timing: Many challenges require precise timing - watch for visual cues

Each mini-game has slightly different mechanics, so experiment and discover the best techniques for each challenge!
