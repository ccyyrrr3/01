How to Play

Basic Controls

  • Aim Use your mouse to aim the cue in your desired direction
  • Power Pull back the cue stick with your mouse to adjust shot power
  • Shoot Release to take your shot

Game Rules

The game follows standard 8-ball pool rules:

  • Players are assigned solids or stripes after the first ball is pocketed
  • Pot all your assigned balls (solids or stripes) before the 8-ball
  • Pocketing the 8-ball before clearing your balls results in a loss
  • Pocketing the cue ball (scratch) forfeits your turn
  • Win by legally pocketing the 8-ball after clearing your assigned balls
