Mini Putt Gem Garden

Sports Golf Minigolf

Putt your way through a beautiful garden filled with shimmering gems and colorful flowers. Navigate challenging courses in this relaxing yet engaging minigolf adventure!

Game Description

Mini Putt Gem Garden is a charming continuation of the Mini Putt series, set in a vibrant garden environment. This beautifully designed minigolf game features lush floral landscapes, sparkling gems, and creative course layouts that will challenge and delight golf enthusiasts.

Each hole offers unique garden-themed obstacles including flower beds, garden paths, water features, and moving elements. You'll need to plan your shots carefully, considering both angle and power to navigate around these challenges and achieve the perfect putt.

With its tranquil garden setting, soothing sound effects, and gradually increasing difficulty, Mini Putt Gem Garden provides a perfect balance of relaxation and challenge. Enjoy this delightful minigolf experience as you aim for the lowest possible score across all the garden-inspired courses.

How to Play

  • Aim: Click and drag to set direction
  • Power: Hold longer for more power
  • Release: Let go to take your shot
  • Reset: R key to restart the hole

Tips:

  • Watch your ball's path to learn the course
  • Use gentle taps for precision near the hole
  • Look for hidden passages and shortcuts
  • Time your shots to pass moving obstacles
