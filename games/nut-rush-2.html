Nut Rush 2: Summer Sprint

Arcade Action Platform

Guide your squirrel through challenging summer levels, collecting nuts and avoiding obstacles in this exciting sequel to the popular Nut Rush game.

Game Description

Nut Rush 2: Summer Sprint brings back our favorite squirrel hero for more adventures in a summer setting. This exciting sequel features new levels, challenges, and power-ups to enhance your gameplay experience.

Navigate through beautiful summer environments filled with obstacles and enemies. Collect as many nuts as possible to score points, and find hidden power-ups to help you on your journey. With simple controls but challenging gameplay, Nut Rush 2 offers a perfect balance for players of all skill levels.

Each level presents unique challenges and surprises. Can you master them all and become the ultimate nut-collecting champion?

How to Play

  • Movement: Arrow keys or WASD
  • Jump: Space bar or Up arrow
  • Special Action: Shift key
  • Pause: P key

Tips:

  • Collect nuts to increase your score
  • Watch out for enemies and obstacles
  • Look for secret paths and hidden items
  • Power-ups can help you overcome difficult sections
