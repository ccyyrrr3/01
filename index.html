How to Play

Game Controls

  • Aim: Move your mouse to position the cue stick
  • Power: Hold and drag back to set shot power
  • Shoot: Release to take your shot
  • Spin: Use the cue ball indicator to apply spin
  • View: Right-click and drag to move the camera angle

Game Rules

8 Ball Billiards Classic follows standard pool rules:

  • Players choose solids or stripes after the break
  • Pot all your assigned balls before the 8-ball
  • Potting the 8-ball before clearing your balls results in a loss
  • Potting the cue ball (scratching) gives your opponent ball-in-hand
  • Call your pocket when shooting the 8-ball

Tips for Winning

  • Plan Ahead: Always think about your next shot and try to position the cue ball accordingly.
  • Control Your Power: Not every shot needs full power. Gentle touches often provide better control.
  • Use Spin Wisely: Apply top, bottom, or side spin to control where the cue ball goes after contact.
  • Safety Plays: Sometimes the best move is to play defensively and leave your opponent with a difficult shot.
  • Practice Mode: Use the practice mode to improve your skills without the pressure of competition.
